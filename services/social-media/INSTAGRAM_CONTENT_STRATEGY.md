## Executive Summary

This document outlines Mitjavila Enterprise's Instagram strategy, including posting frequency, content types, visual guidelines, and posting best practices. Use this as a reference for all Instagram content creation.

**Key Goals:**
- Establish consistent brand presence on Instagram
- Build audience engagement and follower growth
- Drive traffic/interest to Mitjavila Enterprise services
- Maintain professional, trustworthy brand image

## Part 1: Posting Frequency & Schedule

### Recommended Cadence

**For Mitjavila Enterprise: 3-4 posts per week**

**Rationale:**
- High enough for consistent visibility
- Low enough to maintain quality
- Manageable for small business
- Aligns with audience expectations (not overwhelming)

### Sample Weekly Schedule

| Day | Time | Type | Topic |
|-----|------|------|-------|
| **Monday** | 9:00 AM CDT | Text + Image | Weekly motivational or business tip |
| **Wednesday** | 12:30 PM CDT | Carousel | Project showcase or multi-step process |
| **Friday** | 5:00 PM CDT | Image | Team/culture moment or weekend preview |
| **Bonus (Optional)** | Variable | Story or Reel | Time-sensitive updates, behind-the-scenes |

**Why these times?**
- **Monday 9 AM:** Peak engagement for B2B audiences (post-weekend)
- **Wednesday 12:30 PM:** Lunch scroll for broader audience
- **Friday 5 PM:** End-of-week engagement boost
- **Stories/Reels:** Post anytime (not bound by algorithm like feed)

### Flexibility

Adjust timing based on Instagram Insights after 2-4 weeks:
- Check "Total Followers" → "Most Active Times"
- Instagram shows peak hours for YOUR audience
- Adapt schedule to match audience activity

## Part 2: Content Types & Formats

### 1. Feed Posts (Single Image)

**Best For:** Product shots, team photos, motivational quotes, testimonials  
**Frequency:** 2-3x/week  
**Dimensions:** 1080px × 1350px (or any 4:5 ratio)  
**File Size:** Under 8 MB  
**Caption Length:** 100-150 words (punchier than Facebook)

**Example:**
```
🎯 Your business deserves a partner who gets it.

At Mitjavila Enterprise, we've spent years helping businesses 
like yours scale smart. From strategy to execution, we've got your back.

What's your biggest business challenge right now? 👇 
Let's chat.

#SmallBusiness #Growth #MitjavilaEnterprise #BusinessStrategy
```

### 2. Carousel Posts (2-10 Images)

**Best For:** Step-by-step guides, before/after, multiple products, storytelling  
**Frequency:** 1x/week  
**Dimensions:** 1080px × 1350px per image (same as single posts)  
**Max Images:** 10 (but 3-5 is sweet spot)  
**Caption Length:** 150-200 words (can be longer for multi-image)

**Example Flow:**
- **Slide 1:** Hook (attention-grabbing statement)
- **Slide 2-4:** Step-by-step process or showcase
- **Slide 5:** Call-to-action (link, DM, etc.)

**Carousel Caption:**
```
📱 5 Quick Wins You Can Implement This Week

Ready to boost your business? We've put together 5 actionable 
strategies you can start TODAY. Swipe through for the breakdown.

[Slide 1: Title]
[Slide 2: Strategy #1 + image]
[Slide 3: Strategy #2 + image]
[Slide 4: Strategy #3 + image]
[Slide 5: CTA]

Save this for later! 📌

#SmallBusinessTips #BusinessGrowth #Strategy
```

### 3. Reels (15-90 second video)

**Best For:** Attention-grabbing, educational, entertaining, viral potential  
**Frequency:** 1-2x/month (optional, but highly rewarded by algorithm)  
**Length:** 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 30-45 sec)  
**Format:** 1080px × 1920px (9:16 vertical, full screen)  
**Caption:** 50-100 words + hook text in video

**Reel Ideas for Mitjavila:**
- "5 seconds to [business benefit]"
- Time-lapse of a project
- Quick tips or hacks (10 seconds each)
- Team bloopers or culture moments
- Customer testimonials (15-20 seconds)
- Q&A or myth-busting

**Best Practices:**
- Use trending audio (Instagram suggests trending sounds)
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds (text overlay: "Wait for the ending")
- Include captions (people watch muted first)
- Include call-to-action (follow, DM, link in bio)

**Example Reel Script:**
```
[0:00-0:02] Text: "You're Doing This Wrong"
[0:02-0:15] Show common mistake
[0:15-0:30] Show correct approach
[0:30-0:45] Explain why it matters
[0:45-0:60] CTA: "Follow for more tips"

Audio: Trending upbeat track
Caption: "Don't fall for this common mistake. Here's the fix →"
```

### 4. Stories (Ephemeral, 24-hour posts)

**Best For:** Behind-the-scenes, time-sensitive updates, polls/engagement  
**Frequency:** 2-3x/week (optional, not counted toward main feed)  
**Dimensions:** 1080px × 1920px (9:16, full screen)  
**Auto-deletes:** After 24 hours  
**Caption:** Short text overlay + stickers

**Story Ideas:**
- Behind-the-scenes team moments
- "This week we..." updates
- Quick announcements
- Polls/Q&A ("What should we post next?")
- Event coverage (live or recorded)
- Team member spotlights

**Story Best Practices:**
- Keep text minimal (people scan fast)
- Use interactive stickers (polls, questions, quizzes)
- Post at different times (stories don't follow algorithm like feed)
- Highlight favorite stories to "Highlights" for archive
- Use @mentions and hashtags for discovery

## Part 3: Image Requirements & Technical Specs

### Feed Posts (Single & Carousel)

| Spec | Requirement |
|------|------------|
| **Dimension** | 1080px × 1350px (4:5 ratio) |
| **Min Width** | 600px |
| **Max Width** | 1500px |
| **Format** | JPG or PNG |
| **File Size** | Under 8 MB |
| **Color Space** | RGB |
| **Quality** | High (80+ quality for JPG) |

**Why 1080x1350?**
- Fills vertical feed on mobile (standard size)
- Maintains quality on desktop
- Instagram's "sweet spot" for reach/engagement

**Acceptable Variations:**
- Square (1:1 ratio): 1080x1080
- Portrait (3:4 ratio): 1080x1440
- Landscape (3:2 ratio): 1080x720
- **But 4:5 (1080x1350) gets best organic reach**

### Stories

| Spec | Requirement |
|------|------------|
| **Dimension** | 1080px × 1920px (9:16 ratio) |
| **Format** | JPG, PNG, or MP4 |
| **File Size** | Under 100 MB |
| **Duration** | 5-15 seconds (auto) |
| **Color Space** | RGB |

### Reels

| Spec | Requirement |
|------|------------|
| **Dimension** | 1080px × 1920px (9:16 ratio) |
| **Duration** | 15-90 seconds (Instagram recommends 30-45) |
| **Format** | MP4 or MOV |
| **Frame Rate** | 24-60 FPS (30 is standard) |
| **File Size** | Under 100 MB |
| **Audio** | AAC, 48 kHz |
| **Codecs** | H.264 video, AAC audio |

**Reel Creation Tools:**
- **Simple:** Shoot on phone with Instagram app (built-in editor)
- **Medium:** Adobe Premiere Rush, iMovie, CapCut (free)
- **Advanced:** Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro

## Part 4: Caption Strategy

### The Mitjavila Voice

**Tone:** Professional + approachable (not stiff, not casual)  
**Audience:** Small business owners, entrepreneurs  
**Goal:** Educate, inspire, build trust

### Caption Formula

**Hook (1-2 sentences)**
- Start with emotion, question, or benefit
- "Your business deserves..." or "What if you could...?"

**Body (2-3 sentences)**
- Educate or entertain
- Keep it simple and scannable
- Use line breaks for readability

**Call-to-Action (1 sentence)**
- Ask a question, encourage saves, or link to DM
- "What's your biggest challenge?" or "Save this for later"

**Hashtags (15-30)**
- Place below call-to-action
- Mix popular (10K+) + niche (#SmallBusiness) + branded (#MitjavilaEnterprise)
- Use 20-30 hashtags for reach; don't look spammy

### Caption Examples

**Example 1: Motivational**
```
🎯 You're Ready for the Next Level

If you've been thinking about scaling, this is your sign.

The difference between $100K and $1M businesses isn't luck—
it's strategy. It's systems. It's knowing what to focus on.

At Mitjavila Enterprise, we help ambitious founders turn 
ideas into impact.

What's holding you back from your next chapter? 👇

#SmallBusiness #Growth #Entrepreneurship #BusinessStrategy 
#ScaleUp #MitjavilaEnterprise
```

**Example 2: Educational**
```
💡 One Simple Thing That Changed Our Business

We tested 5 different strategies last quarter. 
One stood out—and it's something you can implement TODAY.

[Carousel shows the strategy across 5 slides]

The lesson: Sometimes the best growth hack isn't complex. 
It's focused.

Which strategy will you try first? 👇

#SmallBusinessTips #GrowthHacking #BusinessGrowth 
#EntrepreneurLife #MitjavilaEnterprise
```

**Example 3: Team/Culture**
```
Meet Sarah 👋

Sarah's been with us for 2 years, and honestly, 
we couldn't do what we do without her.

She's the glue that holds our process together—
the one who gets things done right the first time.

This week is all about celebrating the people 
who make Mitjavila tick. 💙

Shoutout to everyone building something great. 
You're crushing it.

#TeamMitjavila #SmallBusiness #TeamCulture 
#FounderLife #MitjavilaEnterprise
```

### Caption Do's & Don'ts

✅ **DO:**
- Ask questions (boosts engagement)
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include a hook in the first 1-2 lines
- Use emojis (1-3 per caption, not overdone)
- Add call-to-action

❌ **DON'T:**
- Write walls of text (use spacing)
- Ask multiple unrelated questions
- Use spam hashtags or banned words
- Copy competitor captions
- Over-emoji (looks unprofessional)
- Use clickbait ("You won't believe...")

## Part 5: Hashtag Strategy

### Hashtag Research

Use these tools to find relevant hashtags:
1. **Instagram Search Bar** — Type keyword, see hashtag suggestions + volume
2. **Later or Buffer** — Built-in hashtag research
3. **Hashtagify.me** — See hashtag volume and related tags
4. **Competitor Posts** — Copy hashtags from competitors' best-performing posts

### Hashtag Mix (For Each Post)

**Total: 15-30 hashtags**

| Type | Count | Examples |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Branded** | 2-3 | #MitjavilaEnterprise #MEBusiness |
| **Niche** (5K-50K followers) | 5-7 | #SmallBusiness #Entrepreneurship |
| **Popular** (100K+) | 3-5 | #SmallBiz #BusinessGrowth |
| **Long-tail** (specific phrases) | 3-5 | #SmallBusinessOwner #ScaleUpYourBusiness |
| **Trending** (if relevant) | 1-2 | Check explore page |

### Placement

Put hashtags **below** the call-to-action:

```
[Main caption text]
[CTA question]

#Hashtag1 #Hashtag2 #Hashtag3...
```

**Why?** Keeps caption readable; hashtags don't count in engagement algorithm.

### Do NOT Use:

❌ Banned hashtags (Instagram blocks these for spam):
- #Follow, #Like, #Comment, #Followback
- Engagement pod hashtags
- Hashtags with "Buy" or "Cheap"

## Part 6: Engagement & Community Management

### Daily Engagement Checklist

✅ **Every morning (9-10 AM):**
- Check new comments on recent posts
- Reply to meaningful comments (personalize replies)
- Check DMs for questions or business inquiries
- Like/comment on 10-15 similar accounts (build community)

✅ **Mid-day (12-1 PM):**
- Check Instagram Insights for performance
- Engage with 5-10 competitor or peer posts
- Respond to any new engagement

✅ **Evening (5-6 PM):**
- Quick check for urgent messages
- Respond to comments from morning post

### Response Policy (Mitjavila)

| Comment Type | Response | Template |
|--------------|----------|----------|
| **Compliment/Thank you** | Auto-respond | "Thanks for the love! 🙌" |
| **Genuine question** | Personal | Answer directly, ask follow-up |
| **Business inquiry** | Personal + DM | "Great question! DM us for details." |
| **Complaint** | Escalate to Jose | Flag for review, respond empathetically |
| **Spam** | Delete | No response needed |

### Building Community

**Growth Strategies:**
1. **Like & comment on niche accounts** (10-15/day)
   - Find accounts in your industry
   - Genuine comments (not "Nice post!")
2. **Use Stories strategically**
   - Tag related accounts (@MitjavilaBusiness)
   - Encourage saves (Save this post 📌)
   - Use location tags (Plano, Texas)
3. **Collaborate with peers**
   - Shoutouts to complementary businesses
   - Tag customers/case studies (ask permission first)
   - Share customer testimonials

**What NOT to do:**
❌ Follow bots or auto-like services  
❌ Use fake engagement services  
❌ Post the same caption repeatedly  
❌ Use pod engagement (Instagram suppresses these)

### Monthly Planning

Use this template in a spreadsheet (Google Sheets, Airtable, or local CSV):

| Week | Date | Type | Topic | Image | Caption | CTA | Hashtags | Posted? | Reach | Engagement |
|------|------|------|-------|-------|---------|-----|----------|---------|-------|------------|

**Columns:**

- **Date:** Specific date to post
- **Type:** Image, Carousel, Reel, Story
- **Topic:** Content subject
- **Image:** Yes/No or URL
- **Caption:** Draft text
- **CTA:** Call-to-action (DM, comment, save, etc.)
- **Hashtags:** Count and primary themes
- **Posted?:** ✅ Done, Pending, Skipped
- **Reach:** People who saw it
- **Engagement:** Likes + comments + shares

### Monthly Review

Every month (or every 4 weeks):
1. Pull analytics from Instagram Insights
2. Record reach and engagement for each post
3. Identify top 3 posts (highest reach/engagement)
4. Note what worked (topic, type, time)
5. Adjust next month's strategy based on data

## Part 8: Analytics & Reporting

### Key Metrics (From Instagram Insights)

| Metric | What It Means | Target |
|--------|--------------|--------|
| **Reach** | # of unique people who saw post | +5-10% week-over-week |
| **Impressions** | Total times post appeared (includes repeats) | 2-3x reach |
| **Engagement** | Likes + comments + shares | 3-5% engagement rate |
| **Saves** | People who saved post | High = valuable content |
| **Followers** | Net new followers | +10-20/month (sustainable) |

## Part 9: Visual Consistency Guidelines

### Brand Colors

Use Mitjavila's brand colors consistently:

| Color | Use |
|-------|-----|
| **Primary Blue** | Backgrounds, text overlays, accents |
| **White/Light Gray** | Text, backgrounds for contrast |
| **Accent Color** | CTAs, highlights, emphasis |

**Avoid:** Mixing too many colors; keep it clean and professional.

### Font & Text Overlays

- **Sans-serif fonts only** (cleaner, more modern)
- **Examples:** Montserrat, Helvetica, Open Sans
- **Size:** Large enough to read on mobile (20pt+)
- **Color:** High contrast (dark text on light, or vice versa)
- **Shadow:** Add drop shadow for text overlay readability

### Photography Style

**What's in:**
- Clean, professional product/team photos
- Bright, natural lighting
- Consistent color grading (slight warm or cool tint)
- People looking at camera (connection)
- Action shots (not staged/stiff)

**What's out:**
- Dark, moody photos
- Stock photo feel (obvious/generic)
- Blurry or low-quality images
- Overly filtered/unrealistic colors
- Awkward posing

### Example Visual Template

Create 2-3 Canva templates (or similar) for consistency:

1. **Quote + background** (motivational posts)
2. **Image + text overlay** (educational posts)
3. **Team photo + badge** (culture posts)

## Checklist: Before You Post

Before hitting "Post," verify:

- [ ] Image is 1080x1350px and high quality
- [ ] Caption is 100-150 words (or 150-200 for carousel)
- [ ] Caption has hook + body + CTA
- [ ] Hashtags are 20-30, mixed type (niche/popular/branded)
- [ ] CTA is clear (comment, save, DM, etc.)
- [ ] Emojis are used sparingly (1-3 max)
- [ ] Post is on-brand (matches visual guidelines)
- [ ] Posting time matches weekly schedule
- [ ] No typos or grammar issues
- [ ] Link in bio is current (if posting a CTA)
