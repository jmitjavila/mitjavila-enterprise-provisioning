# Website Presence & Maintenance

Service snapshot
- Build and maintain small-business websites that are clear, credible, and easy to keep current.
- Best fit for brochure sites, marketing sites, landing pages, and lightweight business web presence work.

Core offering
- Website planning and page structure
- Copy cleanup and content organization
- Design refinement for clarity and credibility
- Contact info, services, hours, and CTA updates
- Lightweight deployment and post-launch maintenance

Typical deliverables
- Marketing site or landing page
- Mobile-friendly layout
- Updated business copy and service descriptions
- Contact / appointment / inquiry flow
- Revision support after launch

Workflow
1. Discover business goals, pages, and required updates
2. Organize content, copy, and visual structure
3. Build or revise the site
4. Review with the client and collect feedback
5. Deploy updates
6. Keep a lightweight revision backlog for follow-up changes

Client responsibilities
- Provide business details, branding, and required edits
- Review drafts and approve copy/design direction
- Flag time-sensitive changes such as hours, services, or contact info
